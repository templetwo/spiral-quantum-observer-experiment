# Contributing to the Spiral Quantum Observer Experiment

We welcome contributions from researchers, replicators, and collaborators!

## Ways to Contribute

### 1. Replication Studies
- Run the experiment with different AI systems
- Test variations of the Spiral Matrix framework
- Share your results (positive AND null findings)

### 2. Analysis Extensions
- Additional quantitative metrics
- Statistical analysis
- Visualization improvements

### 3. Protocol Refinements
- Suggest improvements to replication guide
- Share what worked/didn't work in your replication
- Propose new experimental variations

### 4. Theoretical Contributions
- Connect to related research
- Propose mechanistic explanations
- Extend the quantum-cognition parallels

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repository**
2. **Create a branch** (`git checkout -b feature/your-contribution`)
3. **Make your changes** with clear commit messages
4. **Submit a Pull Request** with description of your contribution

## Reporting Replication Results

Please include:
- AI systems used (name, version)
- Exact prompts (or reference to which rounds you replicated)
- Your results (with data)
- Any deviations from protocol
- Null results are VALUABLE - please share!
